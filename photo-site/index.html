About

My journey started last year when I found a basket hidden in the back of a antique store. It was filled with hundreds of old photographs discarded from a variety of different family albums. It’s quite sad to think that at one point– the image captured a memorable point in someone's life, or memorable enough to deem important to photograph. And now the people had been doomed to be forgotten, taken out of context, and thrown in a bin for fifty cents a piece. Which is why, I decided to create this album online, so that they could always be remembered.
